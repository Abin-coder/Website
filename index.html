01 — About

Researcher & Engineer

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Colorado, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and Natural Language Processing. I am associated with a Language information and computation lab(LINC).

My research is driven by a passion for making AI systems more transparent and trustworthy. I focus on Explainable AI (XAI), multimodal methods, and advancing the capabilities of Large Language Models and Agentic AI systems.

With strong foundations in programming (Python, C++) and deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch), I bring both theoretical rigor and practical engineering to every project.
